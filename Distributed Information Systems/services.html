What We Do

Our list of services includes technology management consulting, data analytics, marketing, sourcing and decision support systems.

Technology Management Consulting

We provide a full range of IT consulting services including technology needs analysis, IT strategic planning, corporate relocations, cyber-security consulting and more. We have helped our clients with the following services:

  • Establishing standards, reducing risks, increasing the efficiency of the product and creating a 3 or 5 technology strategic plan.
  • Assistance with relocating a company and its IT infrastructure to another location or even making it virtual under covid-19 work-from-home guidelines.
  • Negotiating the best rates for clients for an increase in virtual software equipment.
  • Upgrading to the latest and most appropriate technology using the RFP methodology process.
  • Ensuring the client's infrastructure is protected against growing ransomware threats and data breaches, especially in virtual environments.

Data Analytics

DIS services manage data for both operational and analytical purposes. The company analyzes data to drive business processes to improve outcomes via effective decision-making.

The core services we provide are data strategy, operational model design, data management analytics, business intelligence, data science and machine learning.

Marketing

The marketing services we provide help our clients garner more attention towards their products/services and make the most of available opportunities. Logistics

Good sourcing strategies are indispensable to the growth of a technology firm especially when it needs to run an efficient procurement operation. Decision Support Systems

With our interactive information system, we can analyze large volumes of data for making business decisions.
